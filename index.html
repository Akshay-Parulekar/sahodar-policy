Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects user information. The application allows users to create tasks, receive quotations from service providers, communicate with each other, and manage service interactions.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, the application may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Task descriptions and service requests
  • Quotations submitted by service providers
  • Ratings and feedback provided by users

2. How the Application Works

Users can create tasks that are visible to other users who subscribe to specific service categories. Interested users may submit quotations for the task including price and description.

The task creator can view quotation details, but contact information of the quotation sender may remain hidden until the required service fee is paid through the application. Once unlocked, users may communicate with each other to complete the task and update the task status.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To allow users to create and browse tasks
  • To enable quotation submissions
  • To facilitate communication between users
  • To maintain platform security
  • To improve the functionality of the application

4. Data Storage

Application data is securely stored using backend services built with Java Spring Boot. The application database may store user accounts, tasks, quotations, and ratings required for service functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user personal information to third parties. User contact information may only become visible to another user after the required service fee is paid within the application.

6. User Interaction

After contact information becomes visible, users may communicate directly to complete the task. The application is not responsible for agreements or transactions made outside the platform.

7. Ratings and Feedback

Users may provide ratings and feedback after task completion. This helps maintain service quality within the platform.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.

9. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any updates will be reflected on this page with the updated revision date.
